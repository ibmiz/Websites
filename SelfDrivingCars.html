Self-Driving Cars

One application of computing that makes the best contribution to society is the ability to use an array of sensors alongside AI to create systems to help drivers.

This can lead to many benefits such as;

  • Safer Streets
  • Saving Time (and money)
  • Reducing Traffic Jams
  • Using software to reduce emissions
